How to help

Other than staying safe in today's air quality, we must do our part in helping reverse our negative impact on this world. By making changes in how we live we can stop ourselves from polluting our air. A sustainable future without air pollution can only be acheived if all of us are committed to solving the issue at hand.

Transportation

One of the most effective things we can do to reduce air pollution is to reduce is to reduce our usage of vehicles which rely on natural gases. To help the environment, we must change the way we use transportation.

  • Walk or bike for short distance trips
  • Use public transportation
  • Carpool for your commute
  • Combine multiple errands into one trip
  • Avoid prolonged idling of your vehicle
  • When buying a new car, consider one that is the lowest-polluting vehicle or even a zero-emission electric car
  • More importantly, maintain your current vehicle so that its efficiency doesn't decrease

Household

On an everyday level, small positive changes we make can add up to have a bigger impact. Our future depends on our commitment to these actions.

  • Grow plants - The most straightforward yet effective way to reverse air pollution is to grow plants at your own home as well as participate in tree plantations in your community.
  • Don't waste energy - Simply, remember to turn off lights, computers, and electric appliances when not in use.
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle - Reducing your wastage will in turn reduce energy consumption which will help reduce air pollution overall

Step up! Take action!

Raising awareness in your community, school or neighbourhood is crucial in helping reduce air pollution.

How to raise awareness?

Raising awareness could mean any differnt way of conveying the immediate importance of reversing air pollution to the general public. Talking to people, putting up posters, distributing flyers, talking to your local government, starting petitions, and any other way you can reach out to people about this issue is appreciated.
